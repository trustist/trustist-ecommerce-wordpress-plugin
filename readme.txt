Take Open Banking or credit card payments in the UK using TrustistEcommerce

== Description ==

TrustistEcommerce is a powerful payment solution that allows you to easily accept Open Banking or credit card payments in the UK. With TrustistEcommerce, you can securely process payments, manage subscriptions, and integrate with popular platforms like Gravity Forms. Whether you're running an e-commerce store or a subscription-based service, TrustistEcommerce provides a reliable and flexible payment gateway that meets your business needs.

Features:
- Accept Open Banking or credit card payments in the UK
- Seamless integration with Gravity Forms
- Securely process payments and manage subscriptions
- Flexible and customizable payment platform
- Reliable and scalable solution for your business

For more information, please visit our website: [TrustistEcommerce.com](https://www.trustistecommerce.com)

== Installation ==

1. Upload the TrustistEcommerce plugin to your WordPress site.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Configure the plugin settings with your TrustistEcommerce API keys.
4. Start accepting payments and managing subscriptions with TrustistEcommerce.
